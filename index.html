Imagine RIT

Each year at RIT, there is a huge event called ImagineRIT which is an innovative and creative festival throughout campus. Our project involves showing that The Sciences and The Arts are not as opposite as they are taught to be but rather they are fully integrated into one. "Ut totum" is latin for "as a whole." Here we will be posting our progress and showcasing the importance of combining science and arts as a full integration of reality.

Our Projects

Origami

Origami involves significant geometric math to make really awesome paper art. We will be making a paper sculpture and having people come up and make origami.

Recursion/Computer Science

Recursive drawings are based off of a mathematical set of patterns that extend to infinity.Using computer programming we will show how beautiful these patterns are

Lasers and black light art make some pretty cool art. We will be making a luminescent floor piece of Milky Way that will light up when walked upon.
